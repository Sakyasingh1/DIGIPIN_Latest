## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Department of Posts, Government of India ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and processes information through the DIGIPIN API and Reference Implementation Repository.

We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring transparency about our data handling practices.

DIGIPIN is designed to provide Digital Postal Index Number generation and decoding capabilities based on geographic coordinates.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Location Data

When using DIGIPIN-enabled applications or services, geographical location information (latitude and longitude) may be processed to generate or decode DIGIPIN values.

- Location data is not stored for future use.
- No personal identifiers are linked to location data.

### 2.2 Technical Information

We may collect technical information including:

- Device type
- Browser information
- IP address

This information is used solely to ensure proper functioning, security, and performance of services.

### 2.3 Cookies

Cookies may be used solely for security and human verification purposes.

These cookies:

- Do not track user behaviour
- Are used only to protect the service from misuse

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

- Provide accurate DIGIPIN generation and decoding services
- Improve system functionality and performance
- Maintain security and service integrity
- Monitor and analyze service usage

---

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not:

- Sell
- Rent
- Trade

user information with third parties.

Information may be disclosed where required by:

- Applicable law
- Regulatory obligations
- Court orders
- Authorized government personnel performing official duties

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect information, including:

- Encryption of data in transit
- Secure access controls
- Periodic security reviews and assessments
- Protection against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction

---

## 6. Your Choices

Users may deny location access through browser, device, or application settings.

Please note that doing so may limit the ability of DIGIPIN services to generate accurate location-based results.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically for:

- Operational reasons
- Legal requirements
- Security enhancements
- Regulatory compliance

Updated versions will be published with a revised effective date.
